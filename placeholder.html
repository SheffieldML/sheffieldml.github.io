Machine Learning Research Group
University of Sheffield

The Machine Learning group at the University of Sheffield aims to develop algorithms which automatically learn to recognize complex patterns in data, and use these to make intelligent decisions. We have gained an international reputation for research into the development of machine learning techniques and their application to a diverse range of modelling tasks. Our group has established the Open Data Science Initiative and is active in promoting reproducibility of research via good Software Engineering principles.

Our research covers three general areas, with examples:

  1. Learning Algorithms
    • Dimensionality Reduction
    • Probabilistic Modelling
    • Optimisation
    • Statistical Learning
  2. Applications
    • Personalised Health
    • Computational Biology
    • Computational Neuroscience
  3. Ethics of AI
    • Data Legislation
    • Responsible Innovation

We coordinate the Sheffield Machine Learning Research Network. Details of past and future events are available here.
